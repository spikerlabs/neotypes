CompatibilityMappers 

trait CompatibilityMappers extends AnyRef

This trait contains some implicit conversions that are required to enable the collectAs(Col)(session / tx) method on Scala 2.12. It consists of a group of forwarders to the conversions defined in the scala-collection-compat library. These convert from companion objects of the collections, to instances of CanBuildFrom (Factory).

The purpose of this trait, as well of it being mixed in the neotypes.implicits.mappers.results object, is to hide the dependency of the compatibility library to final users. We may as well remove this, and force them to import scala.collection.compat._

The original definitions of the conversions can be found here: https://github.com/scala/scala-collection-compat/blob/master/compat/src/main/scala-2.11_2.12/scala/collection/compat/PackageShared.scala#L47-L86
